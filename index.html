About SmartBusFlow

SmartBusFlow was inspired by the everyday struggle of missing the bus almost every morning during the school week. This frustration led to creating a solution that helps students track their buses in real time. Not knowing how to code was one of our biggest challenges, but overcoming it became our proudest accomplishment.
